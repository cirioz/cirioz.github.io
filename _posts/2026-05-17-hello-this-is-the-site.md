This is a personal scratch pad. Three things will end up here:

1. **Devlogs** — short, dated entries about whatever I'm working on that week
   (often [TouchDesigner]({{ '/blog/category/touchdesigner/' | relative_url }}),
   [drawing]({{ '/blog/category/drawing/' | relative_url }}), or
   [music]({{ '/blog/category/music/' | relative_url }})).
2. **Tutorials** — when I figure something out the hard way and want to save
   the next person (often me, six months later) some time. See
   [tutorials]({{ '/blog/category/tutorials/' | relative_url }}).
3. **Notes** — short pieces that don't fit either of the above. See
   [notes]({{ '/blog/category/notes/' | relative_url }}).

Posts are tagged by *topic* (`programming`, `touchdesigner`, `drawing`, `music`)
and by *format* (`tutorials`, `notes`, `weekly`). A post can have multiple
categories — a TouchDesigner devlog might be `[touchdesigner, weekly]` and
filterable from either side.

If you find anything useful here, it was probably an accident. Welcome anyway.
